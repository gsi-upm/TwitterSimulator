Twitter Simulator

By Álvaro Carrera Barroso
Twitter account: @alvarocarrera
Version: 0.2a
[Requires Java3D]
Twitter simulator based on MASON.

A simulation of the famous social network: Twitter. The behaviour of any user can be modelled to anaylsed the response to external events, such as footbal matches or music concerts.

This model supports the use of non-real time, it is that the simulation has control of how much represents a single step in the simulation and then a concrete period of time and real-life events during the simulation can be scheduled.
